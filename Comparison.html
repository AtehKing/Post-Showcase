Still Media vs Moving Media

Explore the differences between Still Media (e.g., photographs) and Moving Media (e.g., videos).

Still Media

Photographs capture a single moment in time, highlighting details and emotions frozen in place.

Moving Media

Videos tell a dynamic story by capturing motion and sound, providing a vivid experience.
